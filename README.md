# ClawSense AI 🦞

AI-powered Binance trading and opportunity assistant built using the OpenClaw framework.

Project submission for the Binance **#AIBinance** campaign.

---

## Overview

ClawSense AI is designed to help Binance users discover opportunities, manage risk, and learn crypto faster using AI assistance.

The assistant continuously monitors the market and Binance ecosystem to deliver actionable insights.

---

## Problems Solved

Crypto users often struggle with:

- Monitoring the market 24/7
- Discovering Binance earning opportunities
- Managing portfolio risk
- Understanding advanced Binance tools

ClawSense AI solves these challenges using AI automation.

---

## Key Features

### Market Intelligence
AI scans market data and detects trading opportunities.

Examples:

- Volume spikes
- Support & resistance levels
- Futures liquidation zones

---

### Binance Opportunity Scanner

Detects earning opportunities such as:

- Launchpool
- Binance Earn
- Staking
- Promotions & campaigns

---

### Smart Portfolio Manager

Analyzes portfolio risk and suggests:

- Diversification strategies
- DCA opportunities
- Rebalancing signals

---

### AI Crypto Educator

Helps beginners understand Binance tools like:

- Futures Trading
- Dual Investment
- Binance Earn
- Risk management
